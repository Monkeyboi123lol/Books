Diary of a Wimpy Kid Synopsis

"Diary of a Wimpy Kid: Rodrick Rules" is the second book in the popular "Diary of a Wimpy Kid" series by Jeff Kinney. The story follows the misadventures of Greg Heffley, a middle school student, as he navigates the challenges of adolescence. In this installment, Greg's older brother Rodrick takes center stage, causing chaos and embarrassment for Greg. The book explores themes of sibling rivalry, friendship, and the ups and downs of growing up, all told through Greg's humorous diary entries and illustrations. "Rodrick Rules" is a lighthearted and entertaining read that continues to capture the hearts of readers with its relatable characters and witty storytelling.
