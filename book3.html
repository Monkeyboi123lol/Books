Iceberg Synopsis

"Iceberg: The Titanic Disaster" is a compelling historical account that delves into the tragic sinking of the RMS Titanic in 1912. The book provides a detailed narrative of the events leading up to the disaster, the fateful night of April 14th, and the aftermath of the tragedy. It explores the lives of the passengers and crew, the design and construction of the ship, and the factors that contributed to its sinking. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, "Iceberg" offers readers a gripping and emotional journey into one of the most infamous maritime disasters in history, shedding light on the human stories behind the tragedy and the lessons learned from it.
