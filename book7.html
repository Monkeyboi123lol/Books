King of Envy Synopsis

"King of Envy" is a captivating fantasy novel by K. A. Applegate. The story follows the adventures of a young girl named Kira, who discovers she has the power to control the emotions of others. As she navigates her newfound abilities, Kira must learn to manage her powers responsibly and face the consequences of her actions. The book explores themes of power, responsibility, and the complexity of human emotions. "King of Envy" is known for its engaging plot, well-developed characters, and its exploration of moral dilemmas that resonate with readers of all ages.
